# Secret Weapons

Crimsonland features 12 secret weapons (IDs 41-53) with unique unlock conditions. These weapons range from novelty items to extremely powerful alternatives to standard arsenal.

## Secret Weapon List

From `src/crimson/weapons.py`, IDs 41-53 (excluding 46-49 which are UNKNOWN).

### 41. Plague Spreader Gun

**Stats**: Clip 5 · Damage 0.0x · Fire 0.2s · Reload 1.2s · No Ammo Class

**Effect**: Spreads plague infection instead of dealing direct damage

**Behavior**: 
- Zero damage projectiles
- Infects enemies on hit
- Infected enemies spread plague to nearby creatures
- Similar to Plaguebearer perk but weaponized

**Unlock Condition**: Unknown (likely quest-related or achievement)

**Use Case**: Crowd control via infection spread, not direct damage

## Unlock Methods

Secret weapons have various unlock conditions that are not documented in the main game:

### Quest Completion

Some secret weapons unlock after completing all 60 quests or specific hardcore quests.

### Hidden Achievements

Likely achievements like:
- Survive X minutes in Survival
- Reach level X with specific perk combo
- Kill X enemies with specific weapon
- Find hidden areas or easter eggs

### Survival Milestones

Possible triggers:
- Survive 30+ minutes
- Reach level 50+
- Kill 10,000+ enemies in single session
- Achieve specific score thresholds

### Specific Perk Combos

Some weapons may unlock by selecting specific perk combinations:
- Grim Weapon: Related to Grim Deal perk?
- Plague Spreader Gun: Related to Plaguebearer perk?
- Fire Bullets weapon: Related to Fire Bullets bonus?

### Secret Codes or Inputs

Traditional secret weapon unlocks via:
- Typing specific words during gameplay
- Clicking UI elements in specific order
- Pausing at specific times

## Discovery Process

To unlock secret weapons:

1. **Complete all quests**: Especially hardcore chapter (quests 51-60)
2. **Experiment with perk combos**: Try unusual perk combinations
3. **Survival milestones**: Push for long survival times and high levels
4. **Explore UI**: Click unusual menu options, try typing words
5. **Community resources**: Check forums and wikis for discovered unlocks

## Tips for Secret Weapon Hunting

<Tip>
**Check Quest Rewards**: Some quests may silently unlock secret weapons without notification.
</Tip>

<Tip>
**Perk Synergies**: Secret weapons may unlock when specific perk combinations are achieved (e.g., Grim Deal + Final Revenge + Death Clock).
</Tip>

<Tip>
**Survival Endurance**: Many secrets unlock at extreme survival times (20+ minutes, level 30+).
</Tip>

<Warning>
Some secret weapons (Lightning Rifle, Grim Weapon) are statistically worse than standard weapons. They're collectibles, not competitive choices.
</Warning>
